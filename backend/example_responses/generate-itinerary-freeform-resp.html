Day 1: Arrival in the Big Apple

Welcome to New York City! After checking in at your hotel, head to Joe's Pizza for a classic New York slice. Take a leisurely stroll through Madison Square Park and admire the iconic Flatiron Building. Enjoy the evening in Times Square, soaking in the bustling lights and energy of the city.

Day 2: Exploring Manhattan

Start your day with a delicious breakfast at Sarabeth's in Central Park South. Visit the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) to appreciate world-renowned artworks. Take a walk along Fifth Avenue and make a stop at St. Patrick's Cathedral. End the day with a Broadway show in the Theater District.

Day 3: Brooklyn Adventure

Today, venture to Brooklyn and have brunch at Juliana's Pizza for mouthwatering coal-fired pizza. Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge for stunning views of the Manhattan skyline. Explore DUMBO's art scene and visit Brooklyn Bridge Park for a relaxing afternoon by the waterfront.

Day 4: Cultural Immersion

Indulge in a delightful brunch at Serendipity 3, known for its famous Frrrozen Hot Chocolate. Spend the day exploring the diverse neighborhoods of Harlem, with stops at the Apollo Theater and the Studio Museum in Harlem. Enjoy a soulful dinner at a local restaurant to savor authentic flavors.

Day 5: Central Park Retreat

Discover hidden gems in Central Park with a bike rental for a morning ride around the scenic pathways. For lunch, visit Tavern on the Green for a tranquil dining experience in the heart of the park. Explore the Central Park Zoo or simply relax on the lush lawns before heading to Midtown for a shopping spree.

Day 6: Farewell, NYC

On your last day, enjoy a hearty breakfast at Clinton St. Baking Company & Restaurant known for its fluffy pancakes. Take a final stroll through the city streets, perhaps visiting Grand Central Terminal or the Empire State Building. End your trip with a memorable dinner at a rooftop restaurant overlooking the city skyline. Check out of your hotel and cherish the unforgettable memories made in New York City.
